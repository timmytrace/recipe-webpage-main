Funnel cake

Ingredients:


  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 cups milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Vegetable oil, for frying
  • Powdered sugar, for dusting

Preparation steps:

Step 1: In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, granulated sugar, baking powder, and salt until well combined.

Step 2: In a separate bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk, and vanilla extract.

Step 3: Gradually pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients, whisking continuously until you have a smooth batter without any lumps.

Step 4: Fill a large, heavy-bottomed pot or a deep fryer with about 2 inches of vegetable oil. Heat the oil to 375째F (190째C) over medium-high heat.

Step 5: While the oil is heating, prepare a funnel or a squeeze bottle with a wide opening. If using a funnel, cover the spout with your finger
to prevent the batter from pouring out.

Step 6: Once the oil has reached the desired temperature, carefully pour about 1/2 cup of the batter into the funnel or squeeze bottle.

Step 7: Position the funnel or squeeze bottle just above the hot oil and release the batter, moving in a circular motion to create a spiral shape.

Step 8: Fry the funnel cake for about 2-3 minutes on each side or until golden brown, using tongs or a slotted spoon to flip it halfway through. Cook only one funnel cake
at a time to ensure even cooking.

Step 9: Once the funnel cake is cooked, remove it from the oil and transfer it to a plate lined with paper towels to drain excess oil.

Step 10:Repeat the process with the remaining batter, adjusting the heat as needed to maintain the oil temperature.

Step 11:Dust the warm funnel cakes generously with powdered sugar.

Step 12:Serve the funnel cakes immediately while still warm and enjoy!
